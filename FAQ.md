# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is Axonium?
Axonium is a native macOS web browser built with Swift and WebKit. It's designed to be fast, private, and distraction-free.

### Is Axonium free?
Yes, Axonium is completely free to use.

### What macOS version do I need?
Axonium requires macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later.

### Is there an iOS/iPadOS version?
Not yet — and maybe someday! For now, we're focused on making the Mac version the best it can be.

---

## Privacy & Security

### Does Axonium track me?
No. Axonium has no telemetry, no analytics, and no tracking. Your data stays on your device.

### Does Axonium block ads?
Yes! Axonium includes a built-in ad blocker that's enabled by default.

### Where are my passwords stored?
Passwords are stored locally on your Mac, encrypted with AES-256. They sync via iCloud if you enable it.

---

## Features

### Can I import bookmarks from other browsers?
Yes, you can import from Safari, Chrome, and Firefox.

### Does Axonium support extensions?
Not yet. Extension support is currently under consideration.

### What is Quick Open?
Quick Open (⌘K) is a Spotlight-like search that lets you quickly find tabs, history, and bookmarks.

## Troubleshooting

### A website isn't working properly
Try disabling the ad blocker for that site. Some websites don't work well with ad blocking.

### I found a bug, what should I do?
[Open a bug report](../../issues/new?template=bug_report.md) with as much detail as possible.
